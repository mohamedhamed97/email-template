Markting, Business Development & more!

Every law firm relies on new business, happy clients, and referrals to keep the doors open and reduce the temptation to make risky decisions. But, how can you maintain a steady flow of client work without getting overwhelmed in the details of delivering your services?

In this special half-day workshop, three industry experts share strategic systems, processes, and the technology law firms can use to achieve their goals. Including but not limited to; business development, automated marketing tools, converting prospects into clients, innovative intake processes, and generating referrals.
